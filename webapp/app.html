How It Works

NPSketch uses advanced computer vision techniques to analyze hand-drawn images. When you upload a drawing, the system:

  1. Normalizes and preprocesses your image
  2. Detects lines using Hough Transform and edge detection
  3. Compares detected lines to reference patterns
  4. Calculates similarity metrics and generates visualizations
  5. Provides detailed feedback on your drawing accuracy

Perfect for educational tools, drawing practice, or automated quality checking.
