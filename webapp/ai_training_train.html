üìö Training Parameters Explained
Epochs: Number of times the model sees the entire training dataset
  • More epochs = Better learning, but risk of overfitting
  • With augmentation (6x): Use 30-50 epochs minimum
  • Without augmentation: Use 20-30 epochs
Batch Size: Number of samples processed before updating model weights
  • Smaller (2-4): More stable gradients, better for small datasets (< 50 samples)
  • Medium (8): Good balance, works for most datasets (50-200 samples)
  • Larger (16-32): Faster training, needs large datasets (200+ samples)
